Strengthening Texas Communities

Since 2006, Healthy Futures of Texas has worked to reduce unplanned and teen pregnancies through science-based education and advocacy efforts that empower young people, women, and families to make the best decisions for their futures.

We drive progress for a healthier Texas

Every year, we serve 12,000 young people through our programs and millions more through our advocacy efforts. At the state level, our work has maintained or increased millions in funding for preventative healthcare for women, young people and families.

Advocacy
At the Texas legislature, state agencies, school districts and city councils, we're there to ensure the best policies are enacted and budgets for healthcare are preserved and expanded.
Education
We work with youth, parents, schools and community colleges to implement high quality sex ed and clinical referral programs that move the needle on adolescent health outcomes.
Curriculum Design
From traditional multi-lesson facilitator-led curricula for youth and parents to innovative strategies involving youth as curriculum co-developers, we're a leader in developing sex ed that works.
Partnerships
Our partners include government agencies, universities and colleges, school districts and non profits, creating a collaborative network that strengthens communities across the state.
